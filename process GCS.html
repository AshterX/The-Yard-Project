Recycling Processes

At Yard, we are committed to implementing efficient and sustainable recycling processes to reduce waste and promote environmental responsibility.

Collection and Sorting

Our journey begins with the organized collection of waste materials from residents and businesses in designated city areas. Junk collectors, connected through our platform, play a vital role in this process. The collected waste is then transported to our recycling centers.

Recycling Centers

Our recycling centers serve as hubs for waste processing. Here, the collected materials are meticulously sorted into various categories, ensuring that recyclables are separated from non-recyclables. This sorting process optimizes the recycling efficiency and minimizes waste sent to landfills.

Partnerships with Factories

We have established partnerships with recycling factories to ensure that the sorted materials are efficiently processed. These factories convert recyclables into new products and materials, contributing to a circular economy and a reduction in the consumption of new resources.

Environmental Responsibility

Our recycling processes are guided by a strong commitment to environmental responsibility. We adhere to stringent environmental regulations and sustainable practices to minimize our ecological footprint and promote a greener planet.
