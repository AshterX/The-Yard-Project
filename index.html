What We Do

Yard is dedicated to transforming the way we manage waste and promote sustainability. Our comprehensive approach covers various sectors to create a greener, more sustainable future.

Waste Collection and Recycling

We initiate partnerships with existing recycling factories and eventually establish our own recycling facilities to efficiently process waste materials. Our organized collection system connects residents, collectors, and businesses, reducing waste and promoting recycling.

Product Sales

Yard sells recycled materials and eco-friendly products through a network of wholesalers. Our digital platform allows direct product sales to consumers, aligning with our mission to offer sustainable alternatives.

Retail Presence

We open retail shops to enhance market penetration and visibility. These shops offer a wide range of eco-friendly products, providing consumers with accessible and sustainable choices.

Diversification into Other Sectors

We're committed to researching and developing clean energy solutions. Our long-term vision includes entering the energy sector with sustainable energy production, leveraging the expertise gained in waste management operations.
